Detailed Description

Transformation pass to move literals into new clauses if they are independent of remaining literals.

E.g. a(x) :- b(x), c(y), d(y), e(z). is transformed into:

  • a(x) :- b(x), newrel1(), newrel2().
  • newrel1() :- c(y), d(y).
  • newrel2() :- e(z).
